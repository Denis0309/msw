FIFA WORLD CUP 2010

October 14, 2014; 06:05 pm

HEre is what we know about the 2010 World Cup:

- Spain is the WC favorite, what do you think?

- Ivory Coast: for the first time since Roger Milla ‘s Cameroon (Italia 90), an African team seems to be capable of making it to the semi-finals.

- France performance at its last International competition (Euro 2008) was poor to say the least, their race through the 2010 WC was painful and controversial, yet France has been the best performing team at the World Cup Stage for the past decade: World Champ in 98, and runner up in 2006. Will they go passed the group stage?

- the USNT has potential. Since their the last confederation epic come back allowing the team to reach the final and even leading Brazil 2-0 before losing it in the end, the USNT has gained momentum and increase its level of respect around the world. How far will tehy be able to go?

- 2010 is England big comeback at the International Stage. Lead by Fabio Capello, will they finally be able to make it all the way?

- What will be the biggest upset of this 2010 World Cup, the biggest surprised?

What do you think?
